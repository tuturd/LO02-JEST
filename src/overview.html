JEST is a card game, for 3 to 4 players. The player who collects a ‘Jest’ of cards that is worth the most points at the end of the game is the winner !
The game contains 18 cards:
  • 16 suit cards: Each of the 4 suits contains 4 cards: Ace, 2, 3 and 4. The Ace is a special card and can become a 5 in some circumstances.
  • Joker: The Joker is here to cause trouble for the players: Watch out for him!
  • Reference card: Shows the hierarchy of the suits and the ways in which the card combinations score.
